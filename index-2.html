Power Breakfast

How many times do you skip having breakfast (the most important meal of the day!) as you dash to attend your morning meetings or catch that early morning flight? Innumerable times, right! We know the banes of your life and have you covered with free breakfast every morning to charge your fuel.

To keep you rolling round the clock we have got your fitness concerns sorted. With our 24 hour gym armed with professional trainers and the latest equipments, you can surely aim to meet those long forgotten new year resolutions.

Laundry

Early morning meetings, impromptu travels, date nights or jamming with your gang, we want you to deck up the best without hassling over whether you missed to wash your favourite shirt or dress. Self serviced washing machines as well as dedicated laundry services, you can choose your pick.
